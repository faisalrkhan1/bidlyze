FAQ

Common questions

Is my tender data secure?

Yes. Documents are encrypted in transit, processed in real-time, and never stored or used to train AI models. Each analysis is completely isolated and deleted after processing.

What file formats do you support?

PDF, DOCX, and TXT today. Scanned PDFs with OCR and Excel support are coming soon.

How accurate is the analysis?

Bidlyze extracts 95%+ of requirements from well-structured documents. We recommend it as a powerful starting point — always verify critical items with your team.

Do you support Arabic tenders?

Yes. Bidlyze works with English and Arabic documents. French, Spanish, and other languages are coming soon — ideal for GCC and international procurement.

Do I need to create an account?

No. You can analyze your first tenders completely free with no signup. Create an account when you want to save history and unlock more analyses.

What types of tenders work best?

Any government or private sector tender, RFP, RFQ, or RFI. Especially effective for smart city, construction, IT, defence, and healthcare procurement.
